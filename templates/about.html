What is Emend?


xkcd.com (Randall Munroe)

Emend is a service for alerting website owners and authors of grammatical and spelling mistakes found on their site.

Simply subscribe to http://{{handler.host}}/sites/yourdomain.com?atom to get a feed of proposed edits for your site.

To control spam, you will need a Google Account before proposing edits.

Emend was created by John Tantalo based on original ideas by Chris Radcliff and Brad Fults.

Emend is written in Python and runs on Google App Engine.

The code behind Emend is available on GitHub under the MIT License.
