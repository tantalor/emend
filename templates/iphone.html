If you have an iPhone or iPod Touch and the iPhone OS 3.0 software update, you should be able to easily use Emend on the go. Follow these steps to learn how.

Step 1: In Safari, drag the bookmarklet (Emend) to your Bookmarks Toolbar. Then synchronize your iPhone. Make sure you have specified to “Sync Safari bookmarks” in iTunes.

Step 2: On your iPhone, find a page desperately in need of copy editing. Tap and hold to select a word, then drag the sliders to select the desired phrase. Tap “Copy” to save the phrase to your clipboard.

Step 3: Invoke Emend by selecting the bookmarklet, which should be in your “Bookmarks Bar”.

Step 4: Emend should load with the page's URL already filled in. You will probably have to log in first with your Google Account.

Step 5: Focus on the “Original” field and tap “Paste”. Repeat for the “Proposal” field.

Step 6: Alter the proposal to fix the mistake, in this case “mocktail” should be “cocktail”.

Step 7: Tap “Submit proposal”, and you're done!
